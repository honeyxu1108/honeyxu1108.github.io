## Research Projects

**PaCoMo: Controllable Whole-Body Human Motion Generation via Hierarchical Semantic Modeling** — Project Lead  
*Sep. 2025 – Mar. 2026*

- **Overview:** Proposed a unified framework for text-driven, controllable whole-body human motion generation that balances global semantic consistency and fine-grained local control. Introduced a new paradigm supporting both global motion and hierarchical part-level semantic control for precise joint-level manipulation.
- **Text and motion representation:** Built part-aware hierarchical text semantics and a unified whole-body motion representation. Annotated MotionX++ with GPT-4o to produce fine-grained part-level descriptions from rendered motion sequences, and proposed a 642-dim whole-body motion representation including hand and facial expression features.
- **Method:** Part-aware hierarchical VQ-VAE (PaHVQ-VAE) that decomposes whole-body motion into semantically related body parts and encodes them as structured discrete tokens.
- **Model:** PaExFormer (Part-aware Expert Masked Transformer) generates globally consistent whole-body motion under global text while enabling flexible part-level control.
- **Evaluation:** Retrained TMA (Text-Motion Alignment) for whole-body motion evaluation; proposed MPLPE (Mean Per Landmark Position Error) to quantify facial landmark reconstruction quality.
- **Responsibilities:** First author and project lead; responsible for overall system design, dataset construction, model architecture, and core algorithm implementation. Manuscript submitted to **ACM MM 2026** (under review).
